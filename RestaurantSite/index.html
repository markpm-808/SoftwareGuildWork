About us:
Located just a short distance from Waikiki Beach, Cass's Cafe serves coffe, scones, and an array of other drinks and pastries that make us one of the top rated Cafe's in Honolulu. Started up in 2016, we are a local grown cafe that strives to serve our customers with aloha and provide them with a place to relax and unwind.
